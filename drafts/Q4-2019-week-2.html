Fall 2019 Weekly Goal Check-in: Week 2/13

Published Mon 30 September 2019 in Letters

by Melissa Smith Habits Goals Fall 2019 check-in

INTRO

How were last week's habits? What worked? What didn't?

To recap, this quarter I'm concentrating on schedule, sleep, and health. This week, these goals break down into the following tasks and habits:

Schedule: - Brainstorm some ideas to try for my technology-free afternoon break. Whenever summer weather finally ends, this will probably be an afternoon walk, but until then, I’m creating my ‘inclement weather’ routine.

As ridiculous as this sounds, I had a hard time coming up with ideas that genuinely felt like a break that were small enough and enjoyable enough to keep doing them. I need to curate this one more in the following weeks.

  • Set phone alarm for 3 PM break start and end.

I did this one right away. Without it, I don't think I would have remembered to take my break.

  • Start taking a break that's at least 15 minutes long at 3 PM every day.

  • Continue remote co-working with my friend Amy

Sleep: - Continue data gathering using sleep log.

I started using my

  • Exercise every day, as this seems to be one of the biggest factors for me having better sleep. Walks count as exercise.

Health: - Cut out added sugar from breakfast. Eat either oatmeal with berries, plain greek yogurt with berries, savory oatmeal, or plain shredded wheat with berries. - Stop snacking after 10 PM. - Eat an afternoon snack during or after my break. :)

For the week ahead:

Schedule: - Continue afternoon breaks - Continue co-working - On days I'm working but not co-working, embrace that I need a good hour of puttering before I can work. Keep on playing games as is my current habit, but simultaneously move about and do stuff. Attend to my morning routines, tidy things, comb the cats, etc.

Sleep: - Continue exercising every day for better sleep - Write out current bedtime routine. Figure out how long it usually takes. - No coffee after 3 PM (will bump this earlier next week) - Before bed each night, work on writing out, long-hand the thought exercises I've used to fall asleep. Illustrate them. The goal is to really get them steeped into my brain.

Health: - Continue healthy breakfast - Continue not snacking after 10 PM - Continue eating afternoon snack - Begin logging water consumed in Fitbit or MyFitnesPal (accounts are synced) - Set weight lifting goal(s)

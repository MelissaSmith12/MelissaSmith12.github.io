Fall 2019 Weekly Goal Check-in: Week 2/13

Published Mon 30 September 2019 in Letters

by Melissa Smith Habits Goals Fall 2019 check-in

How did last week go? What worked? What didn't?

To recap, this quarter I'm concentrating on schedule, sleep, and health. This week, these goals break down into the following tasks and habits:

Schedule: - Brainstorm some ideas to try for my technology-free afternoon break. Whenever summer weather finally ends, this will probably be an afternoon walk, but until then, I’m creating my ‘inclement weather’ routine. - Start taking a break that's at least 15 minutes long at 3 PM every day. - Set phone alarm for 3 PM break start and end. - Continue remote co-working with my friend Amy

Sleep: - Continue data gathering using sleep log. - Exercise every day, as this seems to be one of the biggest factors for me having better sleep. Walks count as exercise.

Health: - Cut out added sugar from breakfast. Eat either oatmeal with berries, plain greek yogurt with berries, savory oatmeal, or plain shredded wheat with berries. - Stop snacking after 10 PM. - Eat an afternoon snack during or after my break. :)

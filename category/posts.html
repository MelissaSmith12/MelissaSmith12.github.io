Face Masks

Published Wed 06 May 2020 in Posts

by Melissa Smith

In addition to habit coaching and data science, I sew. In this era of face masks, I've dug through my stash of fabrics and am making masks for friends. Masks are available for $5 each if they're being picked up or $6 if I need to ship them. If the mask order is for essential workers, then the masks are free. With the fabrics available, I hope you can find something you love. The whole idea of masks is so stress-inducing, and I hope my masks can help make them slightly joyful.

You can browse the fabrics here and place an order using the form below. In addition to the fabrics I'm offering, you can order any 100% cotton quilting fabric and have it shipped to me (details included in the order form).
